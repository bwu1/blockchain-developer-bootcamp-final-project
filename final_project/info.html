Connecting to the Proper Network

This application is integrated with both Ethereum (Ropsten testnet) and Polygon (Mumbai testnet)


     1. Connecting to Ropsten: In your Web3 Wallet -> Go to Network Settings and switch to "Ropsten"

     2a. Connecting to Mumbai: If you have Coinbase Wallet -> Polygon is by default already added -> Go to Network Settings and switch to "Polygon Mumbai"

     2a. Connecting to Mumbai: If you have MetaMask Wallet -> Add the Polygon Network when prompted by the app -> Go to Network Settings and switch to "Polygon Mumbai"

How Does This App Work?

This DeFi application enables you to spend your self-custodied crypto for real-world goods and services; think of it as converting your crypto wallet into a Debit Card/PayPal account


     1. For users: Automatically converts your crypto into a dollar ($) balance, ready to be immediately spent

     2. For users: Enjoy near gasless transactions due to our seamless integration with Layer 2 scaling technology

     2. For businesses: Leverage blockchain technology to enjoy significantly lower transaction fees compared to traditional payment processors

     3. For businesses: Accept cryptocurrency as payment while getting dollars ($) directly deposited into your bank

What is a Web3 Wallet?

A Web3 wallet allows you to connect to blockchain applications via your internet browser


     1. Current desktop support: MetaMask

     2. Current mobile support: MetaMask & Coinbase Wallet

     3. When creating a wallet account, write down your private key (the list of randomly generated words), and DO NOT SHARE IT WITH ANYONE
